ABOUT US

About our Firm:

Daly, Hamad and Associates, P.L.L.C. was established in 1999 by Jim Daly, CPA and Dan Hamad, CPA. Since inception, the primary objective of the firm has been to provide clients with a high level of personal and quality professional service. The firm performs over 300 audits of Condominium and Homeowners Associations annually and has unparalleled expertise in that field. The firm has consistently received recognition from the AICPA (American Institute of Certified Public Accountants) Peer Review Program for demonstrating that the firm designed and complied with a system of quality control for its accounting and auditing practice, meeting the requirements of the standards established by the AICPA.


About our Partners:

Dan Hamad, Retired

Email: DanHamad@Dalyhamad.com

Dan Hamad has over thirty-five years of auditing, accounting, and tax experience within various industries with a concentration in audits of condominium and homeowners association for the last twenty years. Dan’s auditing expertise and knowledge with condominium and homeowners associations has led to the felony convictions of three individuals in separate incidents for embezzlement of association funds.

Dan Hamad graduated from St. Bonaventure University with an accounting degree in 1982, became a CPA in 1984. He is also an active member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA), the Virginia Society of CPAs, and the Community Association Institute (CAI).

Jim Daly, Retired

Email: JimDaly@Dalyhamad.com

Jim’s professional background encompasses more than forty-eight years of senior, supervisory and management experience in public accounting; and, for the past thirty-seven years, he has focused on the community association industry.

A graduate of the University of Dayton (1969), he is an active member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA), the Virginia Society of CPAs, and the Community Association Institute (CAI). Jim received his CPA designation in 1981.

He spent nine years at KPMG before moving on to Berlin, Seguin, Karam & Ramos. He was a sole proprietor of James M. Daly, CPA from 1978 to 1998.
